A New Culture of Empowerment

The Ogoni Entrepreneurship Summit (OES) is a groundbreaking initiative dedicated to bridging the long-standing mentorship and economic development gap in Ogoniland. Championing the vision of Chief Lesi Maol, the Summit convenes accomplished Ogoni entrepreneurs, professionals, innovators, and development leaders to inspire, mentor, and empower the next generation.

This pilot edition marks the beginning of a new culture of collaboration, skill-building, and structured guidance for Ogoni youths and professionals, ensuring they have the tools and network necessary to thrive in the modern economy.
